    Root canal treatment

    For the treatment

    • New technologies allow us to offer you a safe and painless treatment with stable and long-lasting results.
    • Quality guaranteed by working under a microscope.
    • ​Channel machining.
    • Apexlocator for accurate measurement of root canal length.
    • Sterile work with a rubber dam.​
    • No change in tooth color.

    Why is rubber insulation important?

    • ​​Sterility - no additional infection is introduced into the canal.
    • Patient comfort - no contact with strong antibacterial solutions used to treat canal infection.
    • There is no danger of tools falling into the mouth.

      • Why is working with a microscope important?

    • We find all the channels.​
    • We remove broken tools.​
    • Channel filling control.​​​

    Prosthetic treatment

    For the treatment​

    • When?​​
    • Restoration of missing teeth.
    • Restoration of severely damaged teeth.
    • Why?​​
    • Prevents the displacement and germination of other teeth.
    • Improves hygiene and reduces the risk of caries and gum disease.
    • Improves smile, speech and chewing.
    • ​Provides lip support for a younger face.​
    • The constructions are made of high quality ceramics in a modern laboratory with high aesthetic results.​

    Surgical treatment

    For the treatment​​

    • Removal of untreated teeth.

    Treatment of periodontal diseases

    For the treatment

    • They are caused by bacterial plaque and tartar.​​
    • The bacteria in plaque and tartar produce toxins that irritate the gums and lead to the formation of pockets, melting of the bone, shaking of the teeth.
    • ​If left untreated, they are a major cause of tooth loss.​
    • ​We will prepare an individual plan for prevention and treatment of diseases of your gums.​
